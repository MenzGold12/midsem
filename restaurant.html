Restaurant

Indulge in a superb dining experience at our restaurant, where an outstanding array of culinary options awaits. Our menu encompasses a diverse selection of continental and Ghanaian dishes, presented in an extensive à la carte format. Additionally, our offerings extend to a daily rotating themed buffet.

Whether you prefer a leisurely breakfast, a quick bite, a business meeting over drinks and snacks in the lounge, or a delightful dinner featuring our unique specialties, we cater to your culinary desires. Our flexible approach allows you to dictate your preferences, deciding what, how much, and when you wish to savor our delectable offerings.

Operating from 6:00 am to 10:00 pm, our restaurant accommodates all your dining needs throughout the day. From breakfast and light snacks to full dining experiences, our diverse menu caters to every taste. For those late-night cravings, our night à la carte menu remains available from 10:00 pm to 6:00 am, ensuring that your culinary desires are met around the clock.
